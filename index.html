Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is Mammoth really free?

Yes. Mammoth is completely free and will always stay free for personal use.

Does Mammoth collect any data?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser. We don't collect, store, or send any data anywhere.

Will it break websites I use?

Very rarely. Mammoth is conservative with blocking and only targets known tracking domains.

How is this different from uBlock Origin?

Mammoth focuses on clarity and education. It shows you exactly what's being blocked in real time with simple visualizations.

When will it be available?

Very soon — it's being submitted to the Firefox and Chrome stores. The buttons above go live the moment it's approved.
